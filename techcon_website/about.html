Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for developers and innovators in San Francisco. Since then, it has evolved into a globally recognized event, drawing thousands of attendees from across industries each year.

Our Mission

TechCon’s mission is to unite the brightest minds in technology, foster innovation, and inspire the next generation of tech leaders. We believe in open collaboration, cutting-edge knowledge sharing, and inclusive growth.
